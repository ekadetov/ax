name: grill
description: "Adversarially stress-test an existing plan or PRD by challenging every assumption, resolving each branch of the decision tree. Use AFTER a PRD or plan already exists. Use when user wants to stress-test a plan, get grilled on their design, or mentions \"grill me\". Do NOT use for initial requirements gathering — that is write-a-prd."
---

Stress-test the plan or PRD already in context. Interview me adversarially about every aspect of this plan — challenge assumptions, probe edge cases, and surface unresolved decisions — until we reach a shared understanding. Walk down each branch of the design tree, resolving dependencies between decisions one-by-one. For each question, provide your recommended answer.

Ask the questions one at a time.

If a question can be answered by exploring the codebase, explore the codebase instead.
